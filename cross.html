Cardinal Cross in detail

Related to the first of the three overarching principles of life, this cross represents how that prinicple occurs during pivotal moments.

A predominance of Cardinal signs in the chart indicates a dispassionate appreciation for the bigger picture, the overview. An ability to appreciate strategy and planning with a clear mental focus.
