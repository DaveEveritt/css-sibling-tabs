Esoteric: Virgo ⇧

The esoteric philosophy drawn upon in Alice Bailey’s writing holds that there are three phases of our solar system, under the three rays of aspect. In order, the first phase under Ray 3 is past, we are currently in the second phase with the Sun transmitting Ray 2 energy (hence it’s allocation to Ray 2), and the third phase under Ray 1 is yet to come. The idea here being that the creative power of first ray energy can not yet be expressed in general human culture, yet it still plays a crucial role our individual psychological growth.
